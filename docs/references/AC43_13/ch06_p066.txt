AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 6: CORROSION, INSPECTION & PROTECTION  Section 5: VISUAL CORROSION INSPECTION GUIDE FOR AIRCRAFT

6-66. LAVATORIES AND GALLEYS.
Inspection areas around lavatories, sinks, and
ranges for spillage and corrosion. Pay particular attention to floor area and the area behind and under lavatories, sinks, and ranges.
