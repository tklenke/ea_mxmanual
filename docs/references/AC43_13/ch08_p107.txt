AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 8: ENGINES, FUEL, EXHAUST, AND PROPELLERS  Section 6: PROPELLER TRACKING AND VIBRATION

8-107. GENERAL. To ensure smooth
powerplant operations, first start with a properly-installed propeller. Each propeller should
be checked for proper tracking (blades rotating
in the same plane of rotation). Manufacturer’s
recommendations should in all cases be
followed.
