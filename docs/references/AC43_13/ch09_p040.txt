AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 9: AIRCRAFT SYSTEMS AND COMPONENTS  Section 3: EMERGENCY EQUIPMENT

9-40. SPECIAL INSPECTIONS. Life rafts
in storage or in service shall be unpacked and
thoroughly inspected for mildew whenever
weather or other conditions warrant. The extent of a special inspection will be determined
by the inspector or maintenance chief following a review of the circumstances or conditions
to which the life rafts have been subjected. The
inspector or maintenance chief may direct a
complete overall inspection and inflation test
of the life rafts, regardless of the last date of
inspection, if it is considered that another inspection is warranted.
