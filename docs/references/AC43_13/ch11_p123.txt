AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 9: ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND INSPECTION

11-123. PROTECTION AGAINST PERSONNEL AND CARGO. Wiring must be installed so the structure affords protection
against its use as a handhold and damage from
cargo. Where the structure does not afford
adequate protection, conduit must be used, or a
suitable mechanical guard must be provided.
