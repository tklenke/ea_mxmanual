AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 9: TURNBUCKLES

7-166. TURNBUCKLE INSTALLATION.
(See figure 7-25.) When installing cable system turnbuckles, it is necessary to screw both
threaded terminals into the turnbuckle barrel
an equal amount. It is essential that turnbuckle
terminals be screwed into the barrel so that not
more than three threads on the terminal are exposed. (See figure 7-23A.) On initial installation, the turnbuckle terminals should not be
screwed inside the turnbuckle barrel more than
four threads. (See figure 7-23B.)
