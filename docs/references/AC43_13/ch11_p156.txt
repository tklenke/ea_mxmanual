AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 12: WIRE INSULATION AND LACING STRING TIE

11-156. INSULATION MATERIALS. Insulating materials should be selected for the
best combination of characteristics in the following categories:
a. Abrasion resistance.
b. Arc resistance (noncarbon tracking).
c. Corrosion resistance.
d. Cut-through strength.
e. Dielectric strength.
f. Flame resistance.
g. Heat distortion temperature.
h. Impact strength.
i. Mechanical strength.
j. Resistance to fluids.
k. Resistance to notch propagation.
l. Smoke emission.
AND LACING STRING TIE
m. Special properties unique to the
aircraft.
n. For a more complete selection of insulated wires refer to SAE AS 4372 Aerospace
Wire Performance Requirement and SAE
AS 4373 Test Methods for Aerospace Wire.
