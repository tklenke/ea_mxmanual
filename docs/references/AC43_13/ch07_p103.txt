AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 6: PINS

7-103. COTTER PINS (AN380). Cotter
pins are used for securing bolts, screws, nuts,
and pins. Use AN381 or MS24665 cotter pins
in locations where nonmagnetic material or resistance to corrosion is desired. Cotter pins
should not be reused.
