AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 5: NONDESTRUCTIVE INSPECTION (NDI)  Section 6: RADIOGRAPHY (X-RAY) INSPECTION

5-83. SAFETY. Radiographic safety requirements can be obtained from; the OEM’s
manual, FAA requirements, cognizant FAA
ACO engineers, and radiation safety organizations such as the Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC). Information in radiation safety
publications can be used as a guide to ensure
that radiation exposure of personnel involved
in radiographic operations is limited to safe
levels, and to afford protection for the general
public.
