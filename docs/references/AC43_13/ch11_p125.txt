AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 9: ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AND INSPECTION

11-125. MOVABLE CONTROLS WIRING PRECAUTIONS. Clamping of wires
routed near movable flight controls must be
attached with steel hardware and must be
spaced so that failure of a single attachment
point can not result in interference with controls. The minimum separation between wiring and movable controls must be at least
1/2 inch when the bundle is displaced by light
hand pressure in the direction of the controls.
