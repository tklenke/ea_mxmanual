AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 4: NUTS

7-63. GENERAL. Aircraft nuts are available in a variety of shapes, sizes, and material
strengths. The types of nuts used in aircraft
structures include castle nuts, shear nuts, plain
nuts, light hex nuts, checknuts, wingnuts, and
sheet spring nuts. Many are available in either
self-locking or nonself-locking style. Typical
nut types are shown in table 7-13. Refer to the
aircraft manufacturer’s structural repair manual, the manufacturer’s engineering department, or the FAA, before replacing any nut
with any other type.
