AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 16: WIRE MARKING

11-219. TERMINAL MARKING
SLEEVE AND TAGS. Typical cable markers are flat, nonheat-shrinkable tags. Heatshrinkable marking sleeves are available for
marking wires and cables, and should be inserted over the proper wire or cable and heatshrunk using the proper manufacturer recommended heating tool. (See figures 11-26
and 11-27.)
