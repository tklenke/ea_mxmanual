AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 6: PINS

7-105. QUICK-RELEASE PINS. These
pins are used in some applications where rapid
removal and replacement of equipment is necessary. When equipment is secured with these
pins, no binding of the spindle should be present. Spindle binding could cause the locking
balls to remain in the open position which
could result in the pin falling out under vibration.
