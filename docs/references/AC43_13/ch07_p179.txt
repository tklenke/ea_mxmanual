AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 10: SAFETY METHODS FOR TURNBUCKLES

7-179. GENERAL. Safety all turnbuckles
with safety wire using either the double or single-wrap method, or with any appropriately
approved special safetying device complying
with the requirements of FAA Technical Standard Order TSO-C21. The swaged and unswaged turnbuckle assemblies are covered by
AN standard drawings. Do not reuse safety
wire. Adjust the turnbuckle to the correct cable tension so that no more than three cable
threads are exposed on either side of the turnbuckle barrel.
