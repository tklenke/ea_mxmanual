AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 8: ENGINES, FUEL, EXHAUST, AND PROPELLERS  Section 1: ENGINES

8-13. ENGINE RUN-IN. After an aircraft
engine has been overhauled, it is recommended that the pertinent aircraft engine
manufacturer’s run-in instructions be followed.
concerning engine temperatures and other criteria. Repair processes employed during overhaul often necessitate amending the manufacturer’s run-in procedures. Follow the approved amended run-in procedures in such instances.
NOTE: Do not run up engines on the
ground for long periods of time with
the cowling off. The engine will overheat because cylinder cooling has been
disrupted.
