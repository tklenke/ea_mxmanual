AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 2: FABRIC COVERING  Section 1: PRACTICES AND PRECAUTIONS

2-1. GENERAL. Cotton and Irish linen fabrics were the airframe coverings of choice
from WWI through the 1950’s. However, increases in cost and the short lifespan of natural
fabrics became the driving factors which resulted in almost 100 percent replacement of
original airframe fabrics by man-made,
STC-approved, polyester, and glass filament
fabric.
