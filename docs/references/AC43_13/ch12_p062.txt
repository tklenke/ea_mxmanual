AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 12: AIRCRAFT AVIONICS SYSTEMS  Section 4: PITOT/STATIC SYSTEM

12-62. RELOCATON OF PITOT TUBE. If
pitot tube relocation is necessary, perform the
relocation in accordance with the manufacturer’s recommendations and consider the following:
a. Freedom of aerodynamic disturbances caused by the aircraft.
b. Location protected from accidental
damage.
c. Alignment with the longitudinal axis
of the aircraft when in cruising flight.
