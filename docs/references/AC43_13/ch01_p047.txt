AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 1: WOOD STRUCTURE  Section 4: REPAIRS

1-47. PLYWOOD SKIN REPAIR. Make
extensive repairs to damaged stressed skin
plywood structures in accordance with specific
recommendations from the aircraft manufacturer. It is recommended that repairs be made
by replacing the entire panel, from one structural member to the next, if damage is very
extensive. When damaged plywood skin is repaired, carefully inspect the adjacent internal
structure for possible hidden damage. Repair
any defective frame members prior to making
skin repairs.
A, B, C, D = ORIGINAL DIMENSIONS
