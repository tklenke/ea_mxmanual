AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 1: WOOD STRUCTURE  Section 5: FINISHING WOOD STRUCTURES

1-65. ACCEPTABLE FINISHES. Any
varnish conforming to Federal Specification
TT-V-109, as amended, or other coating approved by the airframe manufacturer or the
FAA is acceptable. Exterior surfaces must be
further protected from the effects of abrasion,
weather, and sunlight. A number of systems
for exterior finishing have STC approval and
are manufactured under a PMA. (See Chapter 2, Fabric Covering.) Low viscosity epoxy
adhesive (meeting the requirements of paragraph 1-4 for that purpose) may be used as an
internal surface finish when subsequent bonding is necessary.
