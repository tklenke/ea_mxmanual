AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 4: INSPECTION OF CIRCUIT-PROTECTION DEVICES

11-51. CIRCUIT BREAKER USAGE.
Circuit breakers are designed as circuit protection for the wire (see paragraph 11-48
and 11-49), not for protection of black boxes
or components. Use of a circuit breaker as a
switch is not recommended. Use of a circuit
breaker as a switch will decrease the life of the
circuit breaker.
