AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 8: INSPECTION AND REPAIR OF CONTROL CABLES AND TURNBUCKLES

7-152. CABLE MAINTENANCE. Frequent inspections and preservation measures
such as rust-prevention treatments for bare
carbon steel cable areas, will help to extend
cable service life. Where cables pass through
fair-leads, pressure seals, or over pulleys, remove accumulated heavy coatings of corrosion-prevention compound. Provide corrosion
protection for these cable sections by lubricating with a light coat of grease or generalpurpose, low-temperature oil.
