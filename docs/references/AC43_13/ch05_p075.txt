AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 5: NONDESTRUCTIVE INSPECTION (NDI)  Section 6: RADIOGRAPHY (X-RAY) INSPECTION

5-75. FILM OR PAPER RADIOGRAPHY. In film or paper radiography, a twodimensional latent image from the projected
radiation is produced on a sheet of film or paper that has been exposed to the unabsorbed
radiation passing through the test piece. This
technique requires subsequent development of
the exposed film or paper so that the latent image becomes visible for viewing.
