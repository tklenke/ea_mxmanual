AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 12: AIRCRAFT AVIONICS SYSTEMS  Section 2: GROUND OPERATIONAL CHECKS FOR AVIONICS EQUIPMENT (ELECTRICAL)

12-15. MARKER BEACON. Marker beacon receivers operate at 75 MHz and sense the
audio signature of each of the three types of
beacons. The marker beacon receiver is not
tunable. The blue outer marker light illuminates when the receiver acquires a 75 MHz
signal modulated with 400 Hz, an amber middle marker light for a 75 MHz signal modulated with 1300 Hz and, a white inner marker
light for a 75 MHz signal modulated with
3000 Hz. The marker beacon system must be
operationally evaluated in VFR when an ILS
runway is available. The receiver sensitivity
switch must be placed in LOW SENSE (the
normal setting). Marker audio must be adequate. Ground test equipment must be used to
verify marker beacon operation. Marker beacon with self test feature, verify lamps, audio
and lamp dimming.
