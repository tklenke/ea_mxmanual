AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 1: WOOD STRUCTURE  Section 4: REPAIRS

1-50. REPAIRS TO DOUBLE CURVATURE PLYWOOD SKIN. The molded plywood necessary for a repair to a damaged plywood skin of double curvature cannot be made
from flat plywood unless the area to be repaired is very small or is of exceedingly slight
double curvature; therefore, molded plywood
of the proper curvature must be on hand before
the repair can be made. If molded plywood of
the proper curvature is available, the repair
may be made using the same procedure as on
single curvature skins.
