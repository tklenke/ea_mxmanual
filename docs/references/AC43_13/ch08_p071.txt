AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 8: ENGINES, FUEL, EXHAUST, AND PROPELLERS  Section 4: REPAIR OF METAL PROPELLERS

8-71. GENERAL. Reject damaged blades
with model numbers which are on the manufacturer’s list of blades that cannot be repaired.
Follow the propeller manufacturer’s recommendations in all cases, and make repairs in
accordance with latest techniques and best industry practices.
NOTE: Title 14 of the Code of Federal Regulations, 14 CFR, part 65 does
not allow an airframe and power
plant mechanic to perform major repairs to propellers.
