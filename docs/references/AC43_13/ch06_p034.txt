AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 6: CORROSION, INSPECTION & PROTECTION  Section 3: CORROSION PROTECTION MEASURES FOR BASIC MATERIALS

6-34.
This treatment consists of boiling magnesium
parts in a solution of sodium dichromate. It
provides good paint base and protective qualities on all standard wrought magnesium alloys
except the magnesium-thorium alloys. Acid
pickling of the magnesium surface prior to application of the dichromate treatment is required if maximum corrosion resistance of the
finish is expected.
