AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 6: CORROSION, INSPECTION & PROTECTION  Section 3: CORROSION PROTECTION MEASURES FOR BASIC MATERIALS

6-35. STANNATE IMMERSION TREATMENT. Stannate immersion treatment deposits
a layer of tin. It is a protective paint base for
magnesium alloy parts which contain inserts
and fasteners of a dissimilar metal such as
brass, copper, or steel. This treatment cannot
be used with parts containing aluminum inserts
or fasteners because the high alkalinity of the
bath attacks the aluminum.
