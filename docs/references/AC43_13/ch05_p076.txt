AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 5: NONDESTRUCTIVE INSPECTION (NDI)  Section 6: RADIOGRAPHY (X-RAY) INSPECTION

5-76. REAL-TIME RADIOGRAPHY.
A two-dimensional image that can be immediately displayed on a viewing screen or television monitor. This technique converts unabsorbed radiation into an optical or electronic
signal which can be viewed immediately or
can be processed with electronic or video
equipment.
