AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 5: WASHERS

7-88. BALL SOCKET AND SEAT
WASHERS (AN950 AND AN955). Ball
socket and seat washers are used in special applications where the bolt is installed at an angle to the surface or when perfect alignment
with the surface is required. These washers
are used together as a pair.
