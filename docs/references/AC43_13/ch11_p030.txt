AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 3: INSPECTION OF EQUIPMENT INSTALLATION

11-30. GENERAL. When installing equipment which consumes electrical power in an
aircraft, it should be determined that the total
electrical load can be safely controlled or managed within the rated limits of the affected
components of the aircraft’s electrical power
supply system. Addition of most electrical
utilization equipment is a major alteration and
requires appropriate FAA approval. The electrical load analysis must be prepared in general
accordance with good engineering practices.
Additionally, an addendum to the flight manual is generally required.
