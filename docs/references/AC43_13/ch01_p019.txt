AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 1: WOOD STRUCTURE  Section 2: HEALTH AND SAFETY

1-19. SANDING IN AREAS OF EXISTING BOND JOINTS AND FINISHES.
Some adhesives used in wooden aircraft construction contain biocides. A commonsense
precaution when machining or sanding existing structure is to wear a respirator to avoid
inhaling dust products. To lower potential fire
hazards avoid using electric sanders around
dope, paints, and adhesives.
