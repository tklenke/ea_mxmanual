AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 6: CORROSION, INSPECTION & PROTECTION  Section 3: CORROSION PROTECTION MEASURES FOR BASIC MATERIALS

6-32. PHOSPHATE RUST-PROOFING.
This process is commercially known as
Parkerizing, Bonderizing, Granodizing, etc.
The coating placed on the part is used to protect steel parts after machining and before
painting.
