AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 15: GROUNDING AND BONDING

11-196. CLEANING. In order to ensure
proper ground connection conductivity, all
paint, primer, anodize coating, grease, and
other foreign material must be carefully removed from areas that conduct electricity. On
aluminum surfaces, apply chemical surface
treatment to the cleaned bare metal surface in
accordance with the manufacturer’s instructions within 4-8 hours, depending on ambient
moisture/contaminate content.
