AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 6: CORROSION, INSPECTION & PROTECTION  Section 5: VISUAL CORROSION INSPECTION GUIDE FOR AIRCRAFT

6-71. ENGINE FRONTAL AREAS.
a. Inspect reciprocating engine cylinder
fins, engine cases, and cooling air ducts for
damage to exterior finish and corrosion.
b. Inspect radiator cooler cores for corrosion.
