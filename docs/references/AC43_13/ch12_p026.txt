AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 12: AIRCRAFT AVIONICS SYSTEMS  Section 2: GROUND OPERATIONAL CHECKS FOR AVIONICS EQUIPMENT (ELECTRICAL)

12-26. RADOME INSPECTION. Inspection of aircraft having weather radar installations should include a visual check of the radome surface for signs of surface damage,
holes, cracks, chipping, and peeling of paint,
etc. Attach fittings and fastenings, neoprene
erosion caps, and lightening strips, when installed, should also be inspected.
