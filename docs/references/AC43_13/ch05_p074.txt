AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 5: NONDESTRUCTIVE INSPECTION (NDI)  Section 6: RADIOGRAPHY (X-RAY) INSPECTION

5-74. LIMITATIONS. Compared to other
nondestructive methods of inspection, radiography is expensive. Relatively large costs and
space allocations are required for a radiographic laboratory. Costs can be reduced considerably when portable x-ray or gamma-ray
sources are used in film radiography and space
is required only for film processing and interpretation. Operating costs can be high because
sometimes as much as 60 percent of the total
inspection time is spent in setting up for radiography. With real-time radiography, operating costs are usually much lower, because
setup times are shorter and there are no extra
costs for processing or interpretation of film.
