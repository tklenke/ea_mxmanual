AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 5: WASHERS

7-87. LOCKWASHERS (AN935 AND
AN936). Lock washers may be used with machine screws or bolts whenever the selflocking or castellated type nut is not applicable. Do not use lock washers where frequent
removal is required, in areas subject to corrosion, or in areas exposed to airflow. Use a
plain washer between the lock washer and
material to prevent gouging the surface of the
metal.
CAUTION: Lock washers are not to
be used on primary structures, secondary structures, or accessories where
failure might result in damage or danger to aircraft or personnel.
