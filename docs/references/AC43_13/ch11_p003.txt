AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 1: INSPECTION AND CARE OF ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS

11-3. FUNCTIONAL CHECK OF
STAND-BY OR EMERGENCY EQUIPMENT. An aircraft should have functional
tests performed at regular intervals as prescribed by the manufacturer. The inspections
or functional check periods should be clearly
stated in the aircraft maintenance manual,
along with the overhaul intervals.
