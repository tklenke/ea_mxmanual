AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 18: CONDUITS

11-248. GENERAL. Conduit is manufactured in metallic and nonmetallic materials
and in both rigid and flexible forms. Primarily, its purpose is for mechanical protection of
cables or wires. Conduit should be inspected
for: proper end fittings; absence of abrasion at
the end fittings; proper clamping; distortion;
adequate drain points which are free of dirt,
grease, or other obstructions; and freedom
from abrasion or damage due to moving objects, such as aircraft control cables or shifting
cargo.
