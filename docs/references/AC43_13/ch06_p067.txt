AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 6: CORROSION, INSPECTION & PROTECTION  Section 5: VISUAL CORROSION INSPECTION GUIDE FOR AIRCRAFT

6-67. BILGE AREAS.
a. Inspect bilge areas for waste hydraulic
fluids, water, dirt, loose fasteners, drill chips,
and other debris.
INSPECTION GUIDE FOR AIRCRAFT
b. Remove any foreign material from
bilge and inspect for corrosion.
