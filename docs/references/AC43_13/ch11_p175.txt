AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 14: TERMINAL REPAIRS

11-175. ATTACHMENT OF TERMINALS TO STUDS. Connectors and terminals
in aircraft require special attention to ensure a
safe and satisfactory installation. Every possibility of short circuits, due to misinstallation,
poor maintenance, and service life, should be
addressed in the design. Electrical equipment
malfunction has frequently been traced to poor
terminal connections at terminal boards.
Loose, dirty, or corroded contact surfaces can
produce localized heating that may ignite
nearby combustible materials or overheat adjacent wire insulation. (See paragraph 11-178)
