AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 5: WASHERS

7-89. TAPER PIN WASHERS (AN975).
Taper pin washers are used with the threaded
taper pin. NAS143 and MS20002 washers are
used with NAS internal wrenching bolts and
internal wrenching nuts. They may be plain or
countersunk. The countersunk washer (designated as NAS143C and MS20002C) is used to
seat the bolthead shank radius, and the plain
washer is used under the nut.
