AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 1: WOOD STRUCTURE  Section 4: REPAIRS

1-43. REPLACING SOLID-TYPE SPARS
WITH LAMINATED-TYPE SPARS. Solid
spars may be replaced with laminated spars or
vice versa, provided the material is of the same
high quality. External reinforcements (plywood or solid) must always be replaced as on
the original member.
