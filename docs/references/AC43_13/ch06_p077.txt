6-77. ELECTRICAL CONNECTORS.
a. Inspect electrical connectors for
breaks in potting compound and corrosion of
pins and wires.
b. If the electrical connector is suspected of having moisture intrusion, disassemble the connector, clean the connector, and
inspect it for corrosion.
