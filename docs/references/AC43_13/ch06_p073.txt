AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 6: CORROSION, INSPECTION & PROTECTION  Section 5: VISUAL CORROSION INSPECTION GUIDE FOR AIRCRAFT

6-73. FLEXIBLE HOSE ASSEMBLIES.
a. Inspect hose assemblies for chafing,
weather-checking, hardening, discoloration,
evidence of fungus, torn weather protective
coatings or sleeves, and corrosion of fittings.
b. Replace any defective, damaged,
twisted, or bulging hoses.
