AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 4: METAL STRUCTURE, WELDING, AND BRAZING  Section 5: WELDING AND BRAZING

4-93. REPAIR BY BOLTED SLEEVE.
Do not use bolted-sleeve repairs on welded
steel-tube structure unless specifically authorized by the manufacturer or the FAA. The
tube area removed by the bolt holes, in this
type of repair, may prove critical.
