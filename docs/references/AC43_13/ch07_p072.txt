AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 7: AIRCRAFT HARDWARE, CONTROL CABLES, AND TURNBUCKLES  Section 4: NUTS

7-72. SHEET SPRING NUTS (AN365).
Sheet spring nuts are commonly called speed
nuts. They are used with standard and sheet
metal self-tapping screws in nonstructural applications. They are used to support line and
conduit clamps, access doors, etc. Their use
should be limited to applications where they
were originally used in assembly of the aircraft.
