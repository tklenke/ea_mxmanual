AC 43.13-1B  Chapter 11: AIRCRAFT ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS  Section 10: SERVICE LOOP HARNESSES (PLASTIC TIE STRIPS)

11-136. SUPPORT. Only string ties or
plastic cable straps in accordance with paragraph 11-158 should be used on service loop
90°
harnesses. A or “Y” type spot tie should
be installed at the harness breakout point on
the harness bundle. Ties should be installed
on service loop harnesses at 4 to 6-inch intervals.
